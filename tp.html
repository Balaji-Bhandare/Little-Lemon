HOME

Welcome to Little Lemon Restaurant! We are a cozy little restaurant that serves up fresh, delicious meals with a focus on using locally sourced ingredients. Our menu features a variety of dishes, from classic comfort food to innovative twists on traditional favorites. Come dine with us and experience the taste of our passion for food.

MENU

Start your meal with our mouth-watering appetizers, like our crispy calamari or our tangy buffalo wings. For entrees, try our signature Little Lemon Chicken, served with fresh vegetables and our homemade lemon sauce. Or indulge in our creamy four-cheese pasta, made with a blend of mozzarella, parmesan, asiago, and romano cheeses. And don't forget to save room for our decadent desserts, like our classic tiramisu or our rich chocolate lava cake.
